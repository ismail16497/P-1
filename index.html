Hotel Management System POS

Introduction:

          The main focus of this project is to provide restaurant with management system to store and manage the data of their customer and employees efficiently.
          In this report I will explain how this management system work, how it stores and manage data and what kind of techniques are used to make this management 
          system without redundancies and error free.
          There are basically 5 entity classes. Waiter, Customer, Chef, Order and MEAL. These entity classes will be used to manage data of staff as well as customers. 
          Main focus also includes to make it easy for the staff to manage their restaurant and its customers.

Objectives

          To design a real-time web application, that will manage our restaurant data and will
            ● Maintain the record of the restaurant data
            ● Output the managed data
          For multiple customers, it will take the different data and will perform CRUD operation store the results in either a database or a text file accordingly

Problem Description

        Problem:
        There are a lot of problem that was being faced by the management of restaurant tomanage staff and its customers. 
        There was problem of inaccuracy, data inconsistency and also very in effective. There was danger of data loss. 
        Too much data at too much places and it also need more man power to manage it.
        

        Problem Solution:
        So, after facing those problem discussed above, we proposed Restaurant Management System to over come those problems 
        and give ease to management and to make the process efficient and effective. Restaurant Management System (RMS) will be 
        implemented in access and it design is based on how customer will be given with good services.

Solution Application Areas

        Our application will mainly target the customer and staff of a Restaurant.
